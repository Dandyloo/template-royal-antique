About Us

A decade of craft, care, and commitment to the finest Ghanaian furniture.

We started with a belief.

Furnish GH was founded in 2014 with a simple idea: that Ghana deserves world-class furniture made right here, by Ghanaian hands. Our founder, Emmanuel Kweku Asante, spent years apprenticing under master carpenters in Kumasi before opening our first workshop in Accra's industrial district.

Craft as philosophy.

We believe furniture should be an investment, not a purchase. Every piece we produce is designed to outlast trends, to age with your family, and to carry the mark of human hands. We do not use mass-produced components. We do not rush. We do not compromise on materials.

A commitment to local.

Our timber is sourced from verified sustainable forests in Ghana. Our upholstery fabrics are woven locally in the Volta Region. Our metal hardware is forged by Accra-based artisans. When you buy from Furnish GH, you invest in a chain of Ghanaian excellence.

What We Stand For

01

Uncompromised Quality

We use only premium-grade local hardwoods, hand-selected for grain and durability. Every joint is hand-finished. Every surface is inspected three times before delivery.

02

Made in Ghana

From raw material to finished piece, everything happens on Ghanaian soil. We are proud to employ, develop, and celebrate local craftspeople.

03

Designed for Life

Our pieces are designed to grow with you — classic enough to stay relevant for decades, bold enough to be noticed. Form and function, always in balance.
